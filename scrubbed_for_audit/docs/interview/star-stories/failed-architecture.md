# STAR Story: Aligning Security and Velocity
**Archetype:** Technical Negotiation (Influence without Authority)
**Keywords:** Stakeholder Management, Consensus, Security, DevEx
**Draft Date:** 2026-06-16

## The Narrative
**Situation:** I initially pursued a custom-built Go proxy for traffic interception.
**Task:** To provide a tailored governance interface, but the long-term maintenance burden became unsustainable.
**Action:** I socialized this realization transparently during a staff sync, reframing the "failure" as a pivot toward sustainability. I led the refactoring effort by pair-programming with the team, moving our logic to a standard OPA sidecar architecture.
**Result:** We reduced our custom codebase by 40%, redirected those engineering hours into high-value product features, and achieved 100% compliance with industry-standard policy-as-code.
